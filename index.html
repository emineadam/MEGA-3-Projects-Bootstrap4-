EASTERN EXPRESS

Türkiye, Jan 9, 2023


Taking the Eastern Express, enjoying some intriguing novel, listening to music, and resting in the wagon-lit while going to Kars in Eastern Anatolia region of Türkiye with stunning landscapes is one of the most popular vacation ideas, especially in winter. Observe the changing geography step by step, and taste this wonderful feeling as you pass through the canyons and villages.

Every day at 17.55 a.m., the Eastern Express, regarded as one of the world's most beautiful railway lines, departs from Ankara train station. After passing Kırıkkale, Kayseri, Sivas, Erzincan and Erzurum, it takes approximately 24 hours to reach Kars and a total of 1933 kilometers are covered during the journey. As you approach Kars , you'll see landscapes blanketed in white snow.
